Manifesto

Three Priorities.
One Direction.

Education should work for every student — not just those who already know how to navigate the system.

01
Making Policy Accessible

Policies like Extenuating Circumstances and AI Declaration exist to protect students — but only if they understand them. I will push for clearer communication, with particular attention to international and non-native English speaking students.

02
Bridging the AI Gap

The University has pushed AI forward — but how to use it, where, and to what extent remains unclear. I've built my own approach and want to be a student-University bridge, so students can use AI confidently and responsibly.

03
Supporting Students Who Struggle Quietly

I took an interruption. For two years I was a student with special status at this University — and I found my way through. Once you've stood in the heavy rain, you want to share your umbrella with the next person who needs it.

🌙 How Zhixing Uses AI for Studying
▸Time management: Ask AI to break a large assignment into a week-by-week schedule.
▸Preview material: Paste a reading and ask for a plain-English summary before diving in.
▸Self-quiz: Ask AI to generate 10 practice questions from your lecture notes.
▸Mark-scheme check: Ask AI to evaluate your draft against published marking criteria.
▸Language support: Use AI for authentic translation of complex policy documents (declare where required).
